Brick Lane, St Peters

Branding, Print, Experiential, Web

Initially designed as an urban design report, the Camperdown Creative Quarter was an opportunity to bring multiple land owners together to form a centralised creative arts district near the Sydney CBD. Branding and estabilishment of identity was necessary to persuade individual land owners to see the benefits of a joint development venture.

As a form of personal developement, I also created the associated stationery and website for the Camperdown Creative Quarter.

Designed at Rare Identity
