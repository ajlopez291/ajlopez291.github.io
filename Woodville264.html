Woodville 264

Branding, Print, Diagrams

Woodville 264 is the proposed development for a warehouse site on Woodville Road in Merrylands. The development required an identity to clearly demonstrate to Parramatta City Council the site's potential as a landmark opportunity for the Woodville Road corridor.

Woodville 264's lifestyle and building design was inspired by the SoHo region of New York City, and as such required branding and identity of a similar aesthetic.
