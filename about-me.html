  • Designed successful tenders for high profile projects within the Urban Design and Planning industry
  • Helped develop a DPS app for a Telstra Conference
  • Learnt HTML, CSS and After Effects
  • Grown a beard (his name is Craig)

      • I've always believed that graphic design is about more than just making things look pretty. Rather, it should be about finding that key message behind something and showing it to the world.
