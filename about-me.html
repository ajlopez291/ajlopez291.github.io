I'm a Graphic Designer with a passion for branding, print design and white space.

With over 5 years of industry experience, I'm currently working in the property and real estate sector, bringing new developments to life through the power of strategic and integrated design.

I've always believed that graphic design is about more than just about aesthetics. Rather, it is about crafting a story that will create a unique connection between brand and audience. It's what got me interested in graphic design as a student and it's what I seek to bring to any project I work on today.
